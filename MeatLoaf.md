Vegan meat loaf
===

### Ingredients
- [ ] 1 Medium Onion
- [ ] 1 cup rolled oats
- [ ] 1 1/2 lbs firm tofu mashed
- [ ] 1/2 cup chopped parsley
- [ ] 1/3 cup soy sauce
- [ ] 1/3 cup ketchup
- [ ] 2 tbsp yellow mustard
- [ ] 1/4 tsp black pepper
- [ ] 1/4tsp garlic powder

### Preparation
Preheat the over to 350Â°. Mix all the ingredients together and press the mixture into a lightly greased load pan. bake for one hour. let cool 15-20mins before trying to remove from the pan. Slice and enjoy with the ketchup.
